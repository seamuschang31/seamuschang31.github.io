Built during my **Software Development Engineer internship at Landseed International Medical Group** (Taoyuan, Taiwan, Jun – Jul 2022).

## What I built

- A **full-stack meeting room reservation system** developed from scratch in 6 weeks.
- Table views with **searching and sorting**, backed by a real-time **SQL Server** database.
- Business logic exposed through **Web APIs**, following the **MVC design pattern** with **Entity Framework**.
- Integrated a third-party **calendar plugin** for intuitive room scheduling.

## What I learned

This internship was a 6-week intensive introduction to the ASP.NET/C# ecosystem — my first experience shipping a production tool end-to-end, from database schema to UI.
