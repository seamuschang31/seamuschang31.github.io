An ongoing practicum project with **Ernst & Young** (Jan 2026 – Present) modeling consumer default and financial ruin at the household level.

## What I'm building

- **Synthetic household-level financial data** calibrated from BLS, Federal Reserve SHED, and BEA datasets, simulating monthly income, spending, and debt trajectories.
- **ML models** (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost) with **SHAP analysis** to predict consumer financial distress over a 12–36 month horizon.
- An **optimization framework** using binary search and simulation to compute personalized safe impulsive spending limits.

## Why it matters

Consumer financial distress rarely comes from one bad decision — it accumulates. By simulating realistic household trajectories, this project quantifies how much discretionary spending a household can sustain before default risk becomes material.
